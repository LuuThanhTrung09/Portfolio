THE TEA STATION STORY

Our Beginning

Founded in 1995, Tea Station began as a humble family venture with nothing more than a passion for exceptional tea and a few cherished teapots passed down through generations. What started in a small corner of our family home has blossomed into a sanctuary for tea lovers across the region.

Our founder, Master Chen Wei, spent years traveling through the misty mountains of Asia, learning ancient tea cultivation techniques from master tea farmers and studying the intricate art of tea blending. These invaluable experiences shaped our philosophy: to honor tradition while embracing innovation.

Our Tea Gardens

Today, we own and operate three pristine tea plantations nestled in the highlands, where the perfect combination of altitude, climate, and soil creates ideal conditions for cultivating premium tea leaves. Each plantation specializes in different varieties:

  • Highland Estate: Home to our finest long black teas and oolong varieties
  • Jasmine Valley: Where we grow delicate green teas and aromatic jasmine flowers
  • Spice Garden: Cultivates herbs and spices for our signature blended teas

All our teas are hand-picked at dawn when the leaves contain maximum flavor and nutrients, then processed using time-honored methods that preserve their natural essence and health benefits.

The Tea Station Experience

Our teahouse is more than just a place to enjoy tea—it's a tranquil retreat from the bustling modern world. Step into our traditional Japanese-inspired space and experience:

  • Authentic Tea Ceremonies: Participate in traditional tea ceremonies led by certified tea masters
  • Tasting Sessions: Explore our seasonal collection and rare vintage teas
  • Meditation Garden: Find peace in our serene outdoor tea garden
  • Educational Workshops: Learn about tea history, brewing techniques, and health benefits

Our Commitment

At Tea Station, we believe that every cup of tea should be an experience—a moment of mindfulness in an otherwise hectic day. That's why we're committed to:

  • Sustainability: 100% organic farming practices with zero pesticides
  • Fair Trade: Direct partnerships with local farmers ensuring fair wages
  • Quality: Every batch is tested and tasted by our expert sommeliers
  • Community: Supporting local artisans and traditional crafts
  • Education: Free workshops and resources for tea enthusiasts

We source our teaware from local craftspeople, use biodegradable packaging, and donate a portion of our profits to environmental conservation efforts in tea-growing regions.
